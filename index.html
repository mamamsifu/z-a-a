Need Accounts Assistance?

At Z.A.A accountants, we support individuals, small and medium sized businesses.

Who We Are

Zena Airahaz & Associates is a family owned business, we provide bookkeeping, payroll, VAT returns, Tax returns*, Accounts preparation* and much more. We specialise on contractors, freelancers, sole trades and SMEs.

We are a team of friendly and honest accountants, who genuinely love what we do and want to relieve the stress of accounting and tax returns for clients who particularly don’t like dealing with this.

We offer fixed prices for all our services** so you don’t have to worry about what you will be paying your accountant!

* these services are provided by our sister company (Prime Accounting Solutions) at no extra cost to the client.

** unless agreed otherwise, we can charge hourly rates.

What we do

Bookkeeping & Accounts Preparation

So you are running a business but don’t have the time and/or the knowledge to keep good financial record and submit proper account reports to HMRC? We provide bookkeeping and accounts preparation & submissions at a fixed price!

Management Reporting

We provide support as need when it comes to tracking your income and expense against budget or previous periods. This is a good add on to have if you want to stay on top of your finances. We provide this service monthly/quarterly/semi-annually/annually, which ever suits the client.

Project Financial Forecasting

We can build bespoke financial models that will help you make better future financial decisions. Whether you want to introduce a new product/service and want to know if its going to be profitable or when it will become profitable to what price to price your product/service.

Payroll

If you have employed people or are paying yourself a salary, you will need to ensure you are deducting correct PAYE and NI, and remitting these promptly to HMRC. We can provide payroll support, were we will calculate all the deduction and what needs to be paid to HMRC and your employees.
